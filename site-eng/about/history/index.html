How Did It Start?

It all started after the major Microsoft crisis. A globally advancing technology trend brought artificial intelligence to the forefront. Tech giant companies were monopolizing day by day, growing, and building a digital monarchy over us, leaving us impoverished in their kingdoms. Technology, the greatest power that material resources have provided to humanity, will soon become inaccessible even with money. We are aware of this problem, and by fulfilling our duty, we embarked on a journey to create a community that raises awareness among those who are not yet conscious enough and guide the field of technology. This power, so deeply integrated into our lives, should not be entrusted to a single entity. If necessary, we must fight for it. This idea is the source of our ambition on this path. Just as there were invasions of lands and oil in the past, there is now a hidden invasion of the digital realm. If no stance is taken against this, we will become technological captives condemned to the consequences of this invasion. For us, this is a situation that is difficult to accept. Therefore, Purplefrog Technology, under the leadership of our founder Mehmet Ece, is dedicated to becoming a mega technology company that fights to reclaim the territories of software, blockchain, and artificial intelligence from other invading powers. The current development of technology has been shaped by humanity learning from its past mistakes. However, the greatest vulnerability behind this progress is the accumulation of unchecked power in the hands of a few companies. Today's digital revolution, though a continuation of the industrial revolution, has also brought along unnoticed risks. The major technological vulnerabilities of the past have taught us valuable lessons. For instance, the 2008 global economic crisis showed not only the fragility of the financial sector but also how a world dependent on technological infrastructure could collapse easily. It was during that time we realized technology is not just a tool but also a center of power. Without the correct interventions in those days, we would not have witnessed the rise of decentralized systems like blockchain today.

Bill Gates once said in a speech: "The future will be shaped by technologies that are extensions of artificial intelligence; this process could create the greatest imbalance of power in human history." This statement clearly emphasizes that technology is not only a means of progress but also a power that poses danger. If this imbalance is not controlled, humanity's future could be driven into uncertainty. Additionally, another major vulnerability of the past was the Cambridge Analytica scandal. This incident revealed how critical data security is and showed that the misuse of personal information could even threaten democracy. Such events prove the need to steer technology more equitably and to increase efforts to raise awareness among people. At Purplefrog Technology, we take an active stance against these issues. Our goal is not only to advance technology but also to ensure that this power remains accessible to everyone and stays within ethical boundaries. If humanity does not guide the technology shaping the future in the right direction, it will not escape becoming slaves of the digital age. Stephen Hawking also warned about technology: "If artificial intelligence is not properly managed, it could be the last invention of humanity." This statement reminds us that technology is not just an opportunity but also a threat if not used correctly. We take this as a roadmap and believe that technology should be shaped as the common heritage of all humanity, not just an elite few. The future of technology continues to be shaped by blockchain, artificial intelligence, and sustainable software. However, in this journey, our greatest responsibility is to avoid repeating past mistakes and to equip society with this awareness. Purplefrog Technology aims not only to lead technology but also to ensure that humanity uses it equally and fairly. It must not be forgotten that technology is merely a tool. How you use it will determine humanity's future. Therefore, while being part of innovation, we must never compromise ethical values.


-Purplefrog Technology
