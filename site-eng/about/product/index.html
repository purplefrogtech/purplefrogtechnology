Turn Your Assets into Games with GDV Games!

GDV, short for "Real-World Asset Games," represents a new generation of games designed to address a future need by targeting a specific player audience. By transforming real-world assets into games, we aim to create a competitive environment through digital challenges, contests, puzzles, and every activity that can be performed one-on-one.

Our goal is to move people away from gambling and similar open-ended, money-focused gaming methods, where losses are controlled by a single central entity. Instead, we aim to create a system where the need to earn money and engage in challenges is based on mutual intellectual and reflexive competition between individuals.

We plan to adapt this new gaming concept to legal regulations and implement its examples globally. Games should not evolve into paid or free versions. Instead, they should be entirely gender-neutral and focused on personal development, determining winning or losing based solely on skill.

By organizing inter-league competitions, we aim to ensure that players face opponents at their deserved skill level and eliminate any form of in-game unfairness.

As a monetary system within the game, we are considering using cryptocurrency smart contracts or, alternatively, a ticketing system. If the ticket system is preferred, players will deposit a certain amount of money to obtain digital tickets for use within the game. These tickets act as a means of storing money. Players can sell their tickets for real money or choose to compete to win more tickets.

In victorious challenges, players will acquire the tickets staked by their opponents, increasing their own ticket pool. This system ensures that earnings are entirely dependent on the outcome of the competition, with no central control over the pot.
